# **Introduction to Poker Coach**
If you're looking to sharpen your Texas Hold 'Em skills, this is the application for you!
This app is designed to help Poker players of every skill level everyone learn Poker at their own pace and can be used as a tool to achieve GTO.

## What Is GTO?
Game Theory Optimal (GTO) is a strategy that optimizes various Poker games. A player that uses GTO can carefully can disguise their strong, medium and weak hands in a way that makes a **player both skilled and unpredictable while also maximizing their earnings.**

[GTO Explained in under 10 minutes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vx0iJ7h_b8o) by Finding Equilibrium.


## The goal of our app
 Instead of playing full hands against opponents, **Poker Trainer** is specifically designed to dro players into a specific scenario mid-hand and asked ot make the best possible decision based only on the information given in front of you.

Each scenario has a **correct answer** determined by poker theory and equity calculations. After you make your choice, our app will tell you if your decision was optimal and explain why (or why not).
Over time, your **correct decision percentage** will reflect how well you understand the game.
